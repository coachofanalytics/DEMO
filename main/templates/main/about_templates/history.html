Diaspora Vision, Mission, and Objectives

Vision

To position Kenyan diaspora as a lead catalyst of positive change

To provide professional services to Kenyan diaspora community and create a conducive environment to promote their growth and prosperity socially, economically and politically and extend such benefits to their country and continent as well as build a cordial relationship with communities of the host nations for common good

Objective 1

To accelerate Kenyan diaspora Unity

Objective 2

To create conducive environment for members to unlock their potential

Objective 3

To be a one-stop-center for Diaspora information

Objective 4

To represent the concerns and issues of members to respective authorities

Objective 5

To support & complement the government efforts to effectively serve its citizens

Motto: Stronger Together

Core Values

Unity
Mobility
Inclusivity
Integrity
